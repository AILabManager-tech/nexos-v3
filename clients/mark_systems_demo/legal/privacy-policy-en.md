## 2. Personal Information Collected

We collect the following types of personal information:

| Data Type | Collection Method | Nature |
|-----------|-------------------|--------|
| Full name | Contact form | Voluntary |

| Browsing data | Analytics cookies | With consent |

---

## 3. Purposes of Collection

In accordance with section 4 of Law 25, we collect your personal information solely for the following purposes:

- **Contact form:** To respond to your inquiries and communicate with you regarding your project.
- **Traffic analysis:** To understand how the platform is used in order to improve it (only with your explicit consent).

No data is collected for direct marketing or resale to third parties.

---

## 4. Consent

In accordance with section 8.1 of Law 25, your consent must be:

- **Manifest:** You must take an affirmative action (check a box, click "Accept").
- **Free:** You may decline without any impact on access to the site.
- **Informed:** Purposes are clearly described before collection.

### Cookies

Our site uses two categories of cookies:

- **Essential:** Required for the site to function (theme, language, session). Active by default.
- **Analytics:** Enabled only after your explicit consent via the consent banner.

You may change your preferences at any time from the consent banner or the Settings page.

---

## 5. Retention Period

In accordance with section 23 of Law 25, your personal information is retained for the following duration:

- **Contact form data:** 24 months after the last interaction.
- **Browsing data (analytics cookies):** 13 months maximum.

Upon expiration of these periods, data is securely destroyed.

## 6. Transfer Outside Quebec

In accordance with section 17 of Law 25, we inform you that certain data may be transferred outside Quebec:

| Third-Party Service | Country | Data Concerned | Purpose |

## 8. Your Rights

Under Law 25, you have the following rights:

- **Right of access:** Obtain communication of your personal information held by Mark Systems.
- **Right of rectification:** Have inaccurate or incomplete information corrected.
- **Right of deletion:** Request the destruction of your information when the purpose of collection has been fulfilled.
- **Right to withdraw consent:** Withdraw your consent at any time for analytics cookies.

### Procedure

To exercise your rights, send an email to **privacy@mark-systems.com** specifying your request. We will respond within 30 days.

---

## 9. Confidentiality Incident

In accordance with section 3.5 of Law 25, any confidentiality incident presenting a risk of serious harm will be:

1. Reported to the Commission d'acces a l'information du Quebec (CAI).
2. Communicated to the affected individuals.
3. Recorded in an incident register.

Incident notification email: **incident@mark-systems.com**
